Fare Category Definitions

Adult: Ages 19 to 64 years.

Youth: Ages 6 to 18 years.

Senior: Ages 65+ years.

Child: Children five years and under ride free.

A physical CC card costs $4, and can be purchased from fare vending machines at bus stations or select service centres, and participating Shoppers Drug Mart locations.

Proof of Payments & Transfers

With just one fare, riders can travel on any Canada Commute vehicle in any direction. Fare is valid for two hours from time of issue or validation.

Customers travelling without a valid fare may be subject to a fine or criminal charge. Always tap your CC card, CC in mobile wallet, credit or debit card every time you board or transfer vehicles.

Fare Policies and General Conditions

Fares must not be altered or reproduced in any fashion.

Fares are non-refundable with the exception of CC e-purse.

Fares will not be replaced if lost or stolen.
