Fare Category Definitions

Adult: Ages 19 to 64 years.

Youth: Ages 6 to 18 years.

Senior: Ages 65+ years.

Child: Children five years and under ride free.

A physical CC card costs $4, and can be purchased from fare vending machines at bus stations, select service centres, or participating Shoppers Drug Mart locations.

Proof of Payment & Transfers

With one fare, riders can travel on any Canada Commute vehicle in any direction. Fare is valid for two hours from time of issue or validation.

Customers travelling without a valid fare may be subject to fine or criminal charge. Always tap your CC card, or use credit/debit, each time you board or transfer.

Fare Policies & Conditions

Fares must not be altered or reproduced in any fashion.

Fares are non-refundable (exception: CC e-purse).

Fares will not be replaced if lost or stolen.
