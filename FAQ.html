Frequently Asked Questions

How do I plan a trip?

Use our Trip Planner section. Enter your origin, destination, date and time, and click “Plan My Trip.”

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and transit passes. See the Fare Chart for details.

Can I track my bus in real-time?

Yes! On the Service Updates page, you’ll find live tracking for all active routes.

Where can I find service alerts?

Service advisories and disruptions appear on the Service Updates page and are emailed if you subscribe.

How do I contact customer support?

Visit our Contacts page under About, or email support@canadacommute.ca for assistance.
