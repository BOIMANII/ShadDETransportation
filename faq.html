Frequently Asked Questions

How often are the stops?

From 7 am to 11 pm, buses arrive every 15–20 minutes. Overnight intervals are 30–45 minutes, varying by route.

Can I bring my luggage?

Yes. Under-seat storage is available but limited during peak times. Larger items may require special handling.

Are there discounts for seniors or students?

Seniors & students ride for $2. Special holiday & event discounts apply universally during select days.

How do I track buses in real time?

Use the Service Updates page for live vehicle tracking across all active routes.

What accessibility features are available?

Buses include ramps, wheelchair spaces, handrails, and tactile paving at stops to assist all riders.

More Rider Questions

Can we bring bikes on the bus?

Yes, all buses include front racks. Most hold two bikes, but availability varies by route and city.

Are pets allowed on the bus?

Pets are not permitted due to allergy concerns, except for service animals. These must be leashed and well-behaved.

How many people can fit on a bus?

Most buses seat around 50 passengers. Including standing room, capacity increases to approximately 80 people.

What happens if buses are cancelled?

Any delays or cancellations will be posted through our app. We run frequent service and aim to minimize disruption.

Code of Conduct

Rider Expectations

  • Refrain from aggressive or intimidating behavior.
  • Pay full and valid fare upon boarding.
  • Provide proof of payment if requested.
  • Be clothed and wear appropriate footwear.
  • No littering on vehicles or at stops.
  • No smoking, vaping, or carrying lit tobacco products.

Prohibited Items & Actions

  • Alcohol or illicit substances.
  • Loud music without headphones.
  • Obstructing aisles or doors.
  • Vandalism or graffiti.
  • Solicitation, panhandling, or spamming.
  • Unsafe items (e.g., weapons).
