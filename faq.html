Frequently Asked Questions

How often are the stops?

From 7 am to 11 pm, buses arrive every 15–20 minutes. From 12 am to 6 am, intervals are 30–45 minutes. Times may vary by region and demand.

Can I bring my luggage?

Yes. There’s under-seat storage, but space is limited during rush hours. Larger items may require special handling.

Are there discounts for seniors, students, or others?

Seniors and students ride for $2. Special holiday and event discounts (e.g., Christmas, sports games) apply to everyone.

What measures ensure accessibility for everyone?

Buses feature ramps, designated wheelchair areas, handrails, and tactile paving at stops to assist riders with mobility or visual impairments.

Can we bring bikes on the bus?

Yes. Each bus has bike racks that hold up to two bikes. Capacity may vary by city and vehicle size.

Are pets allowed on the bus?

Only service animals. For safety and allergy reasons, other pets are not permitted. Service animals must be leashed and well-behaved.

How many people can fit on a bus?

Seating capacity is approximately 50. With standing room, a bus can carry up to around 60 passengers.

What happens if buses are cancelled?

Our app and alerts notify you of delays or cancellations. We run frequent services and will re-accommodate you as soon as possible.
